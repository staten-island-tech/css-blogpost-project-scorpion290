This is a penguin.

A group of penguins in the water is called a raft but on land they’re called a waddle! The black and white “tuxedo” look donned by most penguin species is a clever camouflage called countershading. Penguins may huddle together for several reasons. Penguins evolved to fly underwater. A penguin’s thick feathers aren’t the only way this bird stays warm. Penguins live in many locations and habitats. Contrary to many popular holiday cartoons, you’ll never see penguins and polar bears together in the wild. Penguin feet are adapted to walk long distances. Many male penguins gift female penguins with rocks in order to woo them. According to some animal experts, the penguin is one of the most streamlined animals in the world.

Turkeys can see better than humans. Presidential pardons for turkeys started in 1989. Turkey snoods are for mating. 46 million turkeys are killed each year for Thanksgiving.
